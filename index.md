# Privacy Policy for BitmapCore

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Introduction

BitmapCore is an application designed to help users track, analyze, and discover Bitmaps listed across leading marketplaces in the Bitcoin ecosystem. BitmapCore automatically classifies listed Bitmaps and assigns rarity levels based on the characteristics of each asset.

This Privacy Policy explains how BitmapCore handles information when you use the application.

## Information We Collect

BitmapCore does not require users to create an account.

BitmapCore does not intentionally collect, store, or process personally identifiable information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Physical address
* Government-issued identification numbers

## Market Data

BitmapCore displays publicly available information related to Bitmaps and marketplaces within the Bitcoin ecosystem.

The information displayed by the application is obtained from publicly available sources and is used solely for informational and analytical purposes.

## Wallet Connections

BitmapCore may allow users to connect compatible Bitcoin wallets for the purpose of viewing or interacting with publicly available blockchain-related information.

BitmapCore does not store wallet private keys, seed phrases, passwords, or recovery phrases.

Users are solely responsible for the security of their wallets and credentials.

## Analytics and Technical Data

BitmapCore may collect limited technical information necessary for application functionality, security, performance monitoring, and error diagnosis. This information may include:

* Device type
* Operating system version
* Application version
* Crash reports
* Anonymous diagnostic information

This information is used exclusively to improve the performance and reliability of the application.

## Third-Party Services

BitmapCore may access information provided by third-party services, APIs, or marketplaces in order to display market data and asset information.

BitmapCore is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services.

## Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the integrity and security of the application and its services. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

BitmapCore is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
